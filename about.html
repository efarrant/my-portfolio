About me...

An introduction to coding...

I took the first steps in my career as a web developer in May 2021 after enrolling with SheCodes.I saw the COVID-19 pandemic and consequential lockdown as a time for self-improvement and an opportunity that would likely not come my way again.I first came across coding when I decided to try and get a little understanding of what my partner does for a living, and try and work out why he got so fired up about it! Whilst I dabbled in Python briefly, I found my place with HTML, CSS and JavaScript - the perfect combination of logic, organisation and creativity.

Recent Accomplishments...

I have recently attained my SheCodes Certificates in 'Introduction to Coding' and 'Front End Developent'. During this time I completed a landing page on the various types of diamond cuts available to the customer when choosing an engagement ring, and created my first app, which I entitled The WeatherCat and enjoyed putting a creative twist on a functional project. I am currently completing a third SheCodes course in Responsive Development, and am also building a website for a dear friend for her successful art business.

Away from the screen...

When I'm not at the computer, I love to be outside! A keen endurance runner, I completed the Virtual Manchester Marathon in October 2020 and regularly compete in 10K races, my favourite distance. I also enjoy going on long walks in the countryside, and currently divide the remainder of my free time between coding personal projects and playing my guitar.
