Weather app

This app was coded as part of the SheCodes Plus workshop and features a geolocation button giving the user the weather in their current location, and the 7 day forecast. The search bar also allows the user to check the weather of any city in the world. The view out of the window will depend on the weather in the city that is being searched. Wind speed and humidity data is also provided, along with the ability for the user themselves to change the view out of the window to their preferred image.
